Booking & Scheduling

For grad sessions during spring and summer (peak season), booking 4–8 weeks in advance is ideal. Weekends fill up fast, especially in May and June. If you're booking for Class of 2026, reach out now — spots are going quickly.

For headshots and family sessions, 1–2 weeks is usually fine, though weekends may be tighter. Off-season months (November through February) are more flexible.

Just fill out the contact form or call or text us at (236) 509-2933. Once we've confirmed the details, a 50% deposit holds your session date.

Life happens — rescheduling is no problem as long as we have at least 48 hours notice. Your deposit carries forward to the new date. Last-minute cancellations (under 24 hours) may forfeit the deposit.

Vancouver rain is a real thing. If weather is looking rough, we'll touch base the day before and have a backup plan ready — whether that's a light overcast (which actually photographs beautifully), an alternate covered location, or rescheduling entirely. Your deposit is always protected.

Delivery & Editing

Grad portraits and family sessions are delivered within 5–7 business days. Headshots are delivered within 48 hours. Real estate photography is delivered within 24 hours of the shoot.

You'll receive a link to a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share your images. Files are included at full resolution for printing, plus web-sized versions for social media and digital use.

Yes. If there's a specific image you love but want tweaked — brightness, cropping, etc. — just flag it and we'll sort it out. Minor adjustments are included. Extensive retouching beyond natural skin correction can be quoted separately.

You receive a personal use license, meaning you can print, share, and post your photos freely for personal use. Commercial use (e.g. for business advertising) requires a separate license — just ask. Harbourview Media retains the copyright but will always ask before using your images in our portfolio or marketing.

Real Estate Photography

Pricing is based on property size, number of rooms, and whether exterior shots or special add-ons (like twilight photography) are needed. Call or email to get a quick quote — it's no pressure and usually takes about 5 minutes.

Standard packages include interior and exterior coverage, HDR-blended images, MLS-ready file sizing, and delivery within 24 hours. Add-ons like twilight exteriors, floor plans, or virtual staging can be discussed when you reach out.

Edited photos are delivered within 24 hours of the shoot. Same-day delivery may be available in some cases — just ask when you book.
