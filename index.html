Frequently Asked Questions

When will we receive our photos?

You will receive a "Sneak Peek" within 48 hours, and your full high-resolution gallery is delivered within 4 weeks.

Do you offer a second shooter?

Yes, my Classic and Premium packages include a second photographer to ensure every angle is covered.

Is there a travel fee?

Travel within the Lower Mainland is included. For destination weddings, we simply charge for flight and 2 nights of lodging.

How do we book our date?

A signed contract and a 30% non-refundable retainer are required to officially secure your wedding date.

Do you provide RAW files?

To maintain brand quality, I do not provide unedited RAW files. Every photo you receive is carefully edited for color and tone.

What are your payment terms?

We accept credit card, e-transfer, or bank transfer. The final balance is due 30 days before your wedding date.
