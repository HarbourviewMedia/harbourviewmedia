FAQ

Common Questions

How many photos do we receive?

For a standard 8-hour day, you can expect between 400-600 professionally edited images. Quality always comes before quantity.

What is your turnaround time?

I deliver a sneak peek gallery within 48 hours. Your full high-resolution gallery is delivered within 4 weeks.

Do you travel for weddings?

Absolutely. While I'm based in Vancouver, I frequently travel throughout the Island, Okanagan, and beyond.

How do we secure our date?

A signed contract and a 30% retainer fee are required to officially book your date. The remaining balance is due 30 days before the wedding.
