Common Inquiries

How would you describe your style?

A blend of documentary storytelling and editorial portraiture. I focus on natural colors, clean edits, and real moments rather than forced poses.

Do you offer a second shooter?

Yes, my Full Day collection includes a second photographer, and one can be added to the Signature collection as well.

What is your turnaround time?

You'll receive a handful of "sneak peeks" within 48 hours. Your full high-resolution gallery is delivered within 3–5 weeks.

How do we book?

A signed contract and a 30% retainer fee are required to officially secure your date on my calendar.
