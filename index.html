FAQ

Common Inquiries

How many photos will we receive?

For a standard 8-hour wedding, you can expect 500-700 professionally edited images. Every image delivered is high-resolution and print-ready.

What is your turnaround time?

I deliver a 'Sneak Peek' gallery within 48 hours. Your full online gallery will be ready within 3 to 5 weeks of your wedding date.

Do you travel for weddings?

Absolutely. While I'm based in Vancouver, I frequently travel across the Lower Mainland, Vancouver Island, and the Okanagan.

How do we book our date?

A signed contract and a 30% retainer fee are required to officially secure your date on my calendar.
